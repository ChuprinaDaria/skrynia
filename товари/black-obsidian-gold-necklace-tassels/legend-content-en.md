# Power of Black Obsidian

## Origin

Obsidian is volcanic glass formed during rapid cooling of lava. In ancient cultures, this stone was considered a gift from fire gods, a piece of the underworld that came to the surface.

## Meaning in Slavic Tradition

### Protection
Black obsidian in Slavic beliefs:
- **Reflects negative energy** like a mirror
- **Protects from evil eye** and curses
- **Guards against dark forces** and evil spirits
- **Creates a protective shield** around the owner

### Strength and Grounding
The stone helps to:
- Maintain inner strength in difficult times
- Ground and feel connection with earth
- Make important decisions with clear mind
- Overcome fears and doubts

### Spiritual Purity
Obsidian:
- Cleanses aura from negativity
- Helps to see the truth
- Promotes self-knowledge
- Reveals the hidden

## Combination with Gold

Gold in Slavic tradition symbolizes solar energy, divine light. The combination of black obsidian with gold creates balance:
- **Black stone** – protection, strength, grounding
- **Gold** – light, joy, spirituality

Together they create harmony between dark and light, material and spiritual.

## Tassels as Symbol

Textile tassels in ethnic jewelry:
- **Ward off evil spirits** with their movement
- **Symbolize feminine energy** and creation
- **Bring luck** through their dynamics
- **Green color** of tassel means growth, renewal, life

## Modern Use

Today, obsidian necklace:
- Worn as a powerful talisman
- Used for grounding and balance
- Combined with modern style
- Serves as reminder of inner strength
