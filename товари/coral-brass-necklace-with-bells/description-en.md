# Natural Coral and Brass Necklace with Bells

Luxurious multi-strand necklace made of natural coral and decorated with brass bells – a true masterpiece of Ukrainian ethnic jewelry.

## Features

- **Natural coral** in various shades – from delicate pink to bright red
- **6 brass bells** create a gentle melodic sound when walking
- **Handmade wool pompoms** in cinnamon color
- **Bronze fittings** with ethnic ornament
- **3 strands** create a voluminous effect

## Symbolism

Coral has long been considered a talisman against negative energy and a symbol of feminine beauty. Bells in Ukrainian tradition ward off evil spirits and bring joy. This necklace combines the protective power of coral with the cheerful chime of brass.

## How to Wear

Perfect for:
- Wedding and festive ethnic outfits
- Photo shoots in Ukrainian attire
- Special events and celebrations
- As an accent to boho or folk looks

## Care

- Store in a dry place, avoid moisture
- Protect coral from perfumes and cosmetics
- Wipe with a soft cloth after wearing
- Bells can be polished with special brass cleaner
