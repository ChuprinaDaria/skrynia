# Symbolism of Coral Necklace with Bells

## History

In Ukrainian and Slavic tradition, coral has always held special significance. The red color of coral was associated with life force, ancestral blood, and protection from dark forces.

## Meaning of Coral

### Protection
Coral was considered a powerful talisman that:
- Wards off evil eye and envy
- Protects from illnesses
- Guards women's health
- Shields from negative energy

### Beauty and Femininity
Red coral symbolizes:
- Female strength and beauty
- Motherhood and fertility
- Passion and love
- Youth and life energy

## Bells in Tradition

Brass bells on the necklace have their own symbolism:
- **Ward off evil spirits** with their chime
- **Bring joy** and positive energy
- **Announce the presence** of the wearer
- **Protect** from negativity

## Wedding Tradition

Such necklaces were often worn by young women and brides. Red coral symbolized purity and life force, while bells drove away evil spirits from the young family.

## Modern Meaning

Today, such a necklace:
- Reminds of ancestral connections
- Preserves Ukrainian traditions
- Adds uniqueness to any outfit
- Serves as a powerful talisman for modern women
