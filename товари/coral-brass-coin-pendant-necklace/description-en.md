# Dyed Coral Necklace with Brass Coin Pendants

Bright triple strand necklace made of dyed natural coral in rich red-orange shade, decorated with brass coin pendants with ethnic ornament. Striking boho style jewelry.

## Features

- **Dyed natural coral** – small beads of bright color
- **5 brass coin pendants** with relief ornament and red inlays
- **Decorative brass beads** between corals
- **3 strands** create rich voluminous effect
- **Handmade** – each detail hand-selected

## Symbolism

Coins in Slavic tradition symbolized prosperity and protection. Round pendants with ornament worked as mirrors reflecting negative energy. Combination with red coral enhances protective properties.

- Store separately from other jewelry
- Protect from cosmetics and perfumes
- Wipe with soft cloth
- Brass can be polished with special cleaner
