# Coin Amulets in Slavic Tradition

## Origin of Symbol

Round metal pendants in coin form are one of the oldest amulets. Circle symbolized sun, eternity, wholeness and protection. Metal coins were considered embodiment of wealth and earth's energy.

## Meaning of Coin Amulets

### Protection and Reflection of Negativity
Round metal pendants worked as mirrors:
- **Reflected evil eye** with their shine
- **Mirrored negative energy** back to sender
- **Created protective circle** around owner
- **Scared away dark forces** with chime when walking

### Prosperity and Wealth
Coins symbolized material well-being:
- **Attracted money** by principle of similarity
- **Multiplied wealth** of owner
- **Protected from poverty** and need
- **Ensured stability** in life

## Ornament on Coins

### Sacred Geometry
Patterns on pendants have deep meaning:
- **Central point** – beginning of everything, source
- **Circles around** – protective energy rings
- **Ray weaving** – spreading power to all sides
- **Plant motifs** – growth and prosperity

### Red Inlay
Red stone in coin center:
- **Fire of life** – amulet activation
- **Protector's eye** – sees everything around
- **Amulet's heart** – source of power
- **Ancestors' blood** – connection with family

## Number of Coins

### Five Pendants
Number 5 has special meaning:
- **Five elements** – earth, water, fire, air, ether
- **Five fingers** – creative power of hand
- **Pentagram** – symbol of protection and harmony
- **Balance** – between spiritual and material

## Combination with Coral

### Fire and Earth
Coral and metal coins create powerful combination:
- **Coral** – fire energy, passion, life
- **Brass** – earth's strength, stability, protection
- **Red color** – unites both elements

Together they:
- Amplify each other's energy
- Create double protection
- Attract luck and wealth
- Add life force

## Chime of Coins

### Sound Magic
When walking, coins create special sound:
- **Chime drives away evil spirits** – subtle entities fear metal sound
- **Attracts attention** – announces owner's presence
- **Cleanses space** – sound disperses negativity
- **Brings joy** – melodic sound lifts mood

## Wearing Traditions

### When to Wear
Coin amulets especially beneficial:
- **In travels** – protect on road
- **At market or in trade** – attract customers and profit
- **At celebrations** – enhance festive mood
- **In difficult times** – support and protect

### How to Activate
For amulet to work, it needs:
- Wear on bare skin or close to heart
- Periodically wash under running water
- Keep in sun for energy charging
- Thank for protection

## Gifting

### Coins as Gift
In Slavic tradition:
- **Given at weddings** – for wealth of young family
- **Passed as inheritance** – family amulet and prosperity
- **Given to girls** – for happy marriage
- **Exchanged between friends** – for true friendship

## Difference from Money

### Amulet, Not Currency
Important to understand:
- Coin amulets **are not money** in direct sense
- They are **symbol** of prosperity, not material value
- They are **not spent**, but worn constantly
- They **accumulate energy** of owner
