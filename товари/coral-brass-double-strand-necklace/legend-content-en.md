# Power of Coral in Slavic Tradition

## Origin and Meaning

Coral is not a stone but a fossilized skeleton of marine organisms. In Slavic culture, coral was considered a gift from the sea that brought a piece of water's life force to land.

## Color Symbolism

### Red-Orange Shade
The bright color of coral has deep meaning:
- **Fire of life** – symbolizes life energy and strength
- **Blood of ancestors** – connection with family and protection of lineage
- **Solar energy** – warmth, joy, optimism
- **Passion** – love, desire, emotional strength

## Protective Properties

### Amulet Against Negativity
Coral in Slavic beliefs:
- **Reflects evil energy** as a living organism
- **Protects from evil eye** due to its bright color
- **Warns of danger** – believed that coral darkens when threatened
- **Guards children** – especially worn on babies

### Healing Properties
Our ancestors believed that coral:
- Strengthens health and immunity
- Helps with blood diseases
- Calms nerves and gives peace of mind
- Improves sleep and drives away nightmares

## Feminine Energy

### Coral for Women
Special meaning for women's health:
- **Enhances femininity** and attractiveness
- **Helps in motherhood** – protects during pregnancy
- **Preserves youth** – believed to slow aging
- **Brings love** – worn to attract beloved

## Combination with Brass

### Power of Metal
Brass combined with coral creates balance:
- **Coral** – living energy of water and fire
- **Brass** – power of earth and stability

Together they:
- Ground emotions
- Strengthen protection
- Create harmony of elements
- Amplify each other's energy

## Everyday Amulet

### When to Wear
Coral is especially beneficial:
- **In travels** – protects from dangers
- **At work** – gives confidence and energy
- **In stressful situations** – calms and strengthens
- **At important meetings** – adds charisma

### Wearing Traditions
- Coral worn on bare skin for better contact
- Given to children as first amulet
- Passed from mother to daughter
- Worn with other amulets to strengthen protection

## Modern Use

Today, coral necklace:
- Reminds of connection with nature
- Adds brightness and energy to look
- Serves as amulet in modern world
- Combines traditions with contemporary style
- Gives feeling of life force
