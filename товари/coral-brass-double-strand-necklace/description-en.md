# Natural Coral and Brass Double Strand Necklace with Ties

Elegant double strand necklace made of natural coral in bright shades and decorative brass beads. Minimalist design with textile ties makes it versatile for different looks.

- **Natural coral** in two forms – cylindrical and small beads
- **Brass decorative beads** with patina add ethnic touch
- **2 strands** create refined look
- **Textile ties with tassels** in orange color
- **Adjustable length** – perfect for any neck

## Symbolism

Coral in Slavic tradition is a stone of life force and protection. Its bright red-orange color symbolizes fire, passion and sun energy.

Perfect for:

## Care

- Store separately from other jewelry
- Protect coral from cosmetics and perfumes
- Wipe with soft cloth
- Avoid prolonged contact with water
